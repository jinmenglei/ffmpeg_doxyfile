◆ GIF_DEFAULT_DELAY

#define GIF_DEFAULT_DELAY   10

Major web browsers display gifs at ~10-15fps when rate is not explicitly set or have too low values. We assume default rate to be 10. Default delay = 100hundredths of second / 10fps = 10hos per frame.
