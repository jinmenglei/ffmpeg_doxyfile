\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Callback for checking whether to abort blocking functions. A\+V\+E\+R\+R\+O\+R\+\_\+\+E\+X\+IT is returned in this case by the interrupted function. During blocking operations, callback is called with opaque as parameter. If the callback returns 1, the blocking operation will be aborted.
