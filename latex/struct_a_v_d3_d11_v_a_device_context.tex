Callbacks for locking. They protect accesses to device\+\_\+context and video\+\_\+context calls. They also protect access to the internal staging texture (for \mbox{\hyperlink{hwcontext_8c_abf1b1664b8239d953ae2cac8b643815a}{av\+\_\+hwframe\+\_\+transfer\+\_\+data()}} calls). They do N\+OT protect access to hwcontext or decoder state in general.

If unset on init, the hwcontext implementation will set them to use an internal mutex.

The underlying lock must be recursive. lock\+\_\+ctx is for free use by the locking implementation.
