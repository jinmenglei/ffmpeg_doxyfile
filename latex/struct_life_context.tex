The two grid state buffers.

A 0x\+FF (A\+L\+I\+V\+E\+\_\+\+C\+E\+LL) value means the cell is alive (or new born), while the decreasing values from 0x\+FE to 0 means the cell is dead; the range of values is used for the slow death effect, or mold (0x\+FE means dead, 0x\+FD means very dead, 0x\+FC means very very dead... and 0x00 means definitely dead/mold).
