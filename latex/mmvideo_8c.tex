\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
American Laser Games MM Video \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_decoder}{Decoder}} by Peter Ross (\href{mailto:pross@xvid.org}{\texttt{ pross@xvid.\+org}})

The MM format was used by I\+B\+M-\/\+PC ports of A\+LG\textquotesingle{}s \char`\"{}arcade shooter\char`\"{} games, including Mad Dog Mc\+Cree and Crime Patrol.
