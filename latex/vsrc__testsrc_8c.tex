\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Misc test sources.

testsrc is based on the test pattern generator demuxer by Nicolas George\+: \href{http://lists.ffmpeg.org/pipermail/ffmpeg-devel/2007-October/037845.html}{\texttt{ http\+://lists.\+ffmpeg.\+org/pipermail/ffmpeg-\/devel/2007-\/\+October/037845.\+html}}

rgbtestsrc is ported from M\+Player libmpcodecs/vf\+\_\+rgbtest.\+c by Michael Niedermayer.

allyuv, smptebars and smptehdbars are by Paul B Mahol.
