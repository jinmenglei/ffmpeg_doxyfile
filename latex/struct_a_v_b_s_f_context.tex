\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The bitstream filter state.

This struct must be allocated with \mbox{\hyperlink{group__lavc__core_ga7da65af303e20c9546e15ec266b182c1}{av\+\_\+bsf\+\_\+alloc()}} and freed with \mbox{\hyperlink{group__lavc__core_ga08d53431e76355f88e27763b1940df4f}{av\+\_\+bsf\+\_\+free()}}.

The fields in the struct will only be changed (by the caller or by the filter) as described in their documentation, and are to be considered immutable otherwise.
