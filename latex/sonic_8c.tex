\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Simple free lossless/lossy audio codec Based on Paul Francis Harrison\textquotesingle{}s Bonk (\href{http://www.logarithmic.net/pfh/bonk}{\texttt{ http\+://www.\+logarithmic.\+net/pfh/bonk}}) Written and designed by Alex Beregszaszi

T\+O\+DO\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item C\+A\+B\+AC put/get\+\_\+symbol
\item independent quantizer for channels
\item $>$2 channels support
\item more decorrelation types
\item more tap\+\_\+quant tests
\item selectable intlist writers/readers (bonk-\/style, golomb, cabac) 
\end{DoxyItemize}
