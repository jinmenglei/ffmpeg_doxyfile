\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This bitstream filter splits A\+V1 Temporal Units into packets containing just one frame, plus any leading and trailing O\+B\+Us that may be present at the beginning or end, respectively.

Temporal Units already containing only one frame will be passed through unchanged. When splitting can\textquotesingle{}t be performed, the Temporal Unit will be passed through containing only the remaining O\+B\+Us starting from the first one after the last successfully split frame.
