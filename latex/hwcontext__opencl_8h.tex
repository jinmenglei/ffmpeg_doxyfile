\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A\+P\+I-\/specific header for A\+V\+\_\+\+H\+W\+D\+E\+V\+I\+C\+E\+\_\+\+T\+Y\+P\+E\+\_\+\+O\+P\+E\+N\+CL.

Pools allocated internally are always dynamic, and are primarily intended to be used in Open\+C\+L-\/only cases. If interoperation is required, it is typically required to allocate frames in the other A\+PI and then map the frames context to Open\+CL with \mbox{\hyperlink{hwcontext_8c_a5228c42445cffd10087f0590614edb13}{av\+\_\+hwframe\+\_\+ctx\+\_\+create\+\_\+derived()}}.
