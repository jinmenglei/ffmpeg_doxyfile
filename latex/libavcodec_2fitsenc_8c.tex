\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
F\+I\+TS image encoder

Specification\+: \href{https://fits.gsfc.nasa.gov/fits_standard.html}{\texttt{ https\+://fits.\+gsfc.\+nasa.\+gov/fits\+\_\+standard.\+html}} Version 3.\+0

R\+G\+BA images are encoded as planes in R\+G\+BA order. So, N\+A\+X\+I\+S3 is 3 or 4 for them. Also C\+T\+Y\+P\+E3 = \textquotesingle{}R\+GB \textquotesingle{} is added to the header to distinguish them from 3d images.
