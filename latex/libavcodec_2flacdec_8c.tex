\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
F\+L\+AC (Free Lossless Audio Codec) decoder \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
Alex Beregszaszi 
\end{DoxyAuthor}
\begin{DoxySeeAlso}{See also}
\href{http://flac.sourceforge.net/}{\texttt{ http\+://flac.\+sourceforge.\+net/}}
\end{DoxySeeAlso}
This decoder can be used in 1 of 2 ways\+: Either raw F\+L\+AC data can be fed through, starting from the initial \textquotesingle{}f\+LaC\textquotesingle{} signature; or by passing the 34-\/byte streaminfo structure through avctx-\/$>$extradata\mbox{[}\+\_\+size\mbox{]} followed by data starting with the 0x\+F\+F\+F8 marker.
