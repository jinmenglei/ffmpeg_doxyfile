\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This struct describes the constraints on hardware frames attached to a given device with a hardware-\/specific configuration. This is returned by \mbox{\hyperlink{hwcontext_8c_a80f4c1184e1758150b6d9bc0adf2c1df}{av\+\_\+hwdevice\+\_\+get\+\_\+hwframe\+\_\+constraints()}} and must be freed by \mbox{\hyperlink{hwcontext_8c_a29da7fa7ffa73266d1cbfccb116ed634}{av\+\_\+hwframe\+\_\+constraints\+\_\+free()}} after use.
