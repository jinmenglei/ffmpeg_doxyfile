\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Color\+Matrix 2.\+0 is based on the original Color\+Matrix filter by Wilbert Dijkhof. It adds the ability to convert between any of\+: Rec.\+709, F\+CC, Rec.\+601, and S\+M\+P\+TE 240M. It also makes pre and post clipping optional, adds an option to use scaled or non-\/scaled coefficients, and more...
