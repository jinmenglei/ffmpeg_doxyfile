\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A histogram is an array of H\+I\+S\+T\+\_\+\+S\+I\+ZE \mbox{\hyperlink{structhist__entry}{hist\+\_\+entry}} storing all the energies recorded (with an accuracy of 1/\+H\+I\+S\+T\+\_\+\+G\+R\+A\+IN) of the loudnesses from A\+B\+S\+\_\+\+T\+H\+R\+ES (at 0) to A\+B\+S\+\_\+\+U\+P\+\_\+\+T\+H\+R\+ES (at H\+I\+S\+T\+\_\+\+S\+I\+Z\+E-\/1). This fixed-\/size system avoids the need of a list of energies growing infinitely over the time and is thus more scalable.
