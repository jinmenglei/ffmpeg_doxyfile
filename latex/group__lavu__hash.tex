\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Hash functions useful in multimedia.

Hash functions are widely used in multimedia, from error checking and concealment to internal regression testing. libavutil has efficient implementations of a variety of hash functions that may be useful for F\+Fmpeg and other multimedia applications.
